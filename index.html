Sauron Select Them All

Help Sauron conquer the Middle-Earth

Sauron has a big deep eye that constantly looks over Mordor. He wants to find a powerfull ring to rule them all, one ring to select them, one ring to bring them all and in the darkness bind them.
The most powerfull searching spells of Sauron are querySelector , querySelectorAll , getElementById, getElementsByClassName and getElementsByTagName. Sauron needs your help to use them wisely and find the ring.

The Middle-Earth

In the middle-earth a company of friends are on a journey to bring something powerfull and magical to Mount Doom and destroy it! This magical circular artifact has great powers. A bunch of Hobbits, a dwarf, an elf and two humans are part of the company.
The hobbits are Frodo Baggins , Sam Gamgee , Peregrin Took , Meriadoc Brandybuck and Fredegar Bolger . The elf is Legolas Greenleaf , the three men are Aragorn, Boromir and the wizard Gandalf the Grey, the dwarf is Gimli .
During the journey the company splits, Frodo and Sam Gamgee have to reach mount Doom togheter with the help of a strange and untrustworthy creature called Gollum that once possessed the disc-shaped thing and wants it back.
Frodo and Sam finally reach mount Doom to destroy the round object almost risking their life. Finally in front of the eternal flame of Mount Doom Frodo launch the full-moon-shaped thing in the lava to destroy it!
